Platform overview

Three modules that power a modern crowdfunding marketplace.

01

Campaign origination

A self-serve campaign builder allowing founders, developers, and project sponsors to structure raises under Regulation Crowdfunding, Regulation A, or exempt-market dealer frameworks. Built-in compliance tooling handles KYC, accredited investor verification, and subscription agreement generation without requiring the issuer to engage outside counsel for every campaign.

02

Investor marketplace

A discoverable campaign feed allowing retail and accredited investors to browse active raises by category, stage, target raise, and minimum investment.
